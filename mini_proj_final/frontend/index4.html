About Us

JPFM is a medical organization that offers various healthcare services, including X-ray imaging services. X-ray imaging is a diagnostic tool used by medical professionals to visualize the internal structures of the body, such as bones and organs. JPFM's X-ray imaging services use advanced technology to capture high-quality images of the body, which can help doctors diagnose and treat various medical conditions, including fractures, infections, and tumors. The organization employs experienced and licensed radiologic technologists who operate the X-ray equipment and ensure that patients receive safe and accurate imaging procedures. If you need to undergo X-ray imaging, you can contact JPFM healthcare services to schedule an appointment with their imaging department. During your appointment, a radiologic technologist will guide you through the imaging process and answer any questions you may have. The resulting images will be interpreted by a board-certified radiologist, who will provide your doctor with a report of the findings to help guide your treatment plan.
